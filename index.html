LEGION OF DEFY

Legion of Defy is a Crypto digital and Marketing services utility token on the Binance Smart Chain Network.

Legion of Defy will offer greater value & safety to crypto projects by the delivery of important digital and marketing services through a trusted partner network and pay passive busd Rewards against the angagement of these services.

LEGION PARTNERS CHANNELS

INFORTANT IMPORMATION

  • Customer Rewards Allocation:

    There is a 10% Legion of Defy token allocation that has been designated to a customer rewards wallet. Purchases of any Legion of Defy and/or Network Partner services above a min. threshold will be rewarded with a monthly rebate against the cost of engaged services paid in Legion of Defy Rewards.

    These Legion of Defy tokens will be able to be claimed from a NFT Rewards contract/dApp with the number of tokens able to be claimed per customer per month defined by a tier structure based on their annual Product & services spend.

  • Partner Rewards Allocation:

    There is a 10% Legion of Defy token allocation that has been designated to a Partner rewards wallet. This will provide a rewards framework paid in Legion of Defy tokens for our Network service partners. This is to ensure that we attract the very best services providers within the defy industry and can enhance the breadth of services we can deliver our engaged customers/audience over time. These Legion of Defy tokens will be able to be claimed from a NFT Rewards contract/dApp with the number of tokens able to be claimed per Partner per month defined by a tier structure based on the amount of sales revenue generated through the Legion of Defy network.

INFORTANT IMPORMATION

  • Staking Platform:

    There is a 10% Legion of Defy token allocation that has been designated to pay staking rewards. Staking will not be available to all holders and only those who purchase and hold a Legion of Defy Staking NFT will be able to connect to the Legion of Defy Staking dApp and earn 100% APR. A 3 month staking period lock will apply. A 25% early withdrawal fee will be incurred against tokens withdrawn during staking lock periods.

  • Lottery Prize Pool:

    There is a 10% Legion of Defy token allocation that has been designated to provide Legion of Defy Lottery NFT Holders a 1-10 chance to win a weekly BUSD prize pot. This weekly BUSD Prize pot will be generated by the BUSD rewards earned on this 10% Legion of Defy token allocation and will compound over time due to the buy and sell tax distribution to this wallet which will buy more Legion of Defy tokens that will remain in this Lottery Prize pool rewards wallet. Weekly Lottery Prize Pool Revenue Split

    ⚫ 70% of the weekly BUSD Lottery Pool Will be Shared equally between NFT Holders who win weekly lottery Draw
    ⚫ 30% of the weekly BUSD Lottery Pool will be distributed to the Core Team as Salary

    The winners will be drawn via live AMA using the Duck Race App to randomise the draw to make it fair for all. An in-house Lottery Pool dApp will be developed to automate and streamline the process. You must hold a Legion of Defy Lottery NFT to eligible to win a share of the weekly prize pool. The Number of winners/shares is dependant on the number of NFT's sold at the end of each week. Max: 50 Shares when all 500 Lottery NFT's have been sold
